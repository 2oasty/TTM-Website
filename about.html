About The Business

Since 1990, The Time Machine has been providing quality service to customers located not only in the USA, but also to those around the world. The Time Machine was founded by professional watchmakers with a commitment to providing a wide selection of Name Brand watches, low prices and great service in one convenient location. We pride ourselves on having extremely low prices, fast shipping and great customer service.

We are a Factory Authorized Service Center for all Casio and Royal electronic products, Casio watches, and Anne Klein watches. We are a fully computerized center capable of providing quick responses to your questions regarding your repairs and part orders. We perform both in and out of warranty repairs while offering one of the fastest turn-around times in the industry. We have trained technicians to service your needs and to answer your technical questions. In addition to the service available, we maintain an extensive inventory of parts.

Feel free to send us an email at service@timemachineusa.com, call (818) 249-6007, or come visit us in-store if you have any questions. We look forward to serving you!
