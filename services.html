Services

Here at The Time Machine, we provide service for Casio and Royal electronic products, Casio watches, Anne Klein watches, and other name brand watches. In order to have your item serviced, please send it to us in a well-padded package. Ensure to send it in a way that is insured and trackable. Please include a note containing your name, address, phone number, email address, and a brief explanation of the problem you are having with the item. Note: Do NOT provide any of the original packaging, unless you do not care for it, as we will dispose of it.

If your item is under warranty, please also include a copy of your receipt/proof of purchase. Otherwise, we will send you a written estimate by mail or email. Our turn-around time is one to two weeks, but it can vary depending on availability of parts and your response time to our estimate.

All repairs are guaranteed for 90 days. Should a failure occur, which you believe is related to the repair, please return the merchandise with a copy of our invoice and a brief note explaining the problem.

If you have any further questions about our service, feel free to contact us at (818) 249-6007 or email us at service@timemachineusa.com

Services Overview

Battery Replacement

If your watch is no longer keeping time like it once was, it might be its battery. Battery replacements are fairly easy and quick. Water pressure tests are always conducted after a battery replacement to ensure your watch has a good seal.

Band Repair

If you have a band you really like and don't want to buy a new one, let us help you and fix your broken strap! Whether the band has broken links, pins, or clasps, we're able to replace them and make your band as good as new.

Deep Cleaning

It's normal for watches to get scratched and dirty over time. If you feel like your watch needs a little TLC, bring it in so we're able to give it a deep clean and buff and polish it. When you get your watch back, it'll look as if you just bought it!
