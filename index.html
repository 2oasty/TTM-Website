The Process

STEP 1

Ship your watch

Pack your watch in a well-padded package and send it to us. Include a note containing your name, address, phone number, email address, and an explanation of the problem.

STEP 2

Pay in-person/over the phone

Come into the store or give us a call to pay for your order. You will need to know the Job Number that we will have provided as well as the Model Number of your item.

STEP 3

We'll repair your watch

Our skilled team of technicians will get right to work on your watch, working in a timely and proficient manner to ensure you get the quality you deserve!
